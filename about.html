Admissions

Enrollment Requirements

We welcome students from all backgrounds to apply to Taburnal Learning Center. To enroll, please prepare the following:

  • Copy of birth certificate (PSA)
  • Report card from the last school year
  • 2x2 ID picture (2 copies)
  • Good moral certificate (for transferees)
  • Completed enrollment form (available at the office)

Enrollment Period

Enrollment opens every April and runs through June for the upcoming academic year. Late enrollees may still be accommodated based on available slots.

Contact Our Registrar

For further inquiries or assistance with enrollment, feel free to visit our school or contact our registrar's office during office hours. We're here to help!
